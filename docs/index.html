Why a security consortium?

Staying safe on the new web requires holistic planning, dedicated measures and collaborative efforts across the whole ecosystem. Incentives are high for adversaries to act against the greater good. Writing secure code is not enough.

Our vision is to drive standards and collaboration to improve operational security practices in the Ethereum ecosystem by focusing on planning and best practice measures.
